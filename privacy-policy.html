Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Information We Collect

At hsyed.dev, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. We collect information in the following ways:

Contact Information

  • Name and email address when you contact us through our contact form
  • Phone number if you provide it for SMS notification services
  • Company information if you're representing a business
  • Project details and requirements you share with us

Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address and browser information for security purposes
  • Website usage analytics to improve our services
  • Cookies for essential website functionality

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Responding to your inquiries and providing customer support
  • Delivering software development and SMS notification services
  • Communicating project updates and important information
  • Improving our website and services based on user feedback
  • Ensuring the security and proper functioning of our systems
  • Complying with legal obligations and protecting our rights

Data Protection & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure servers and databases with restricted access
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Employee training on data protection best practices
  • Compliance with applicable data protection regulations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To provide services you've requested (e.g., SMS delivery providers)
  • To comply with legal requirements or court orders
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud or abuse
  • In connection with a business transfer or merger

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service provider
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience:

  • Essential cookies for website functionality
  • Analytics cookies to understand website usage
  • Preference cookies to remember your settings

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Post the updated policy on our website
  • Update the "Last Updated" date
  • Notify you via email if you're an active client
  • Provide reasonable notice before changes take effect

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Questions About This Policy?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please don't hesitate to contact us.
