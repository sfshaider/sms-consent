1. Program Description

Our SMS notification service provides real-time alerts about slot availability for HyperVision COD subscriptions and related services. This is an automated messaging system designed to notify you immediately when slots become available.

2. Consent & Opt-in

By providing your mobile phone number and checking the consent box, you:

  • Expressly consent to receive SMS messages from our slot monitoring service
  • Agree that this consent is not required as a condition of purchase
  • Confirm that you are the authorized user of the mobile phone number provided
  • Acknowledge that you are at least 18 years old or have parental consent

3. Message Types & Frequency

Types of Messages:

  • Slot Availability Alerts: Immediate notifications when monitored slots become available
  • System Status: Updates about monitoring service status
  • Error Notifications: Alerts about monitoring issues or failures
  • Service Updates: Important changes to the monitoring service

Message Frequency:

  • Frequency varies based on slot availability
  • Could range from no messages to several per day during high-activity periods
  • Emergency or critical system messages may be sent regardless of normal frequency limits

4. Opt-out Instructions

You may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by:

  • Text STOP: Reply with "STOP" to any message to immediately unsubscribe
  • Text UNSUBSCRIBE: Alternative opt-out keyword
  • Contact Support: Email or contact us directly (see contact information below)
Important: After texting STOP, you will receive one final confirmation message. No further messages will be sent unless you re-subscribe.

5. Help & Support
