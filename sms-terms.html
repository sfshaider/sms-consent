5. How to Opt Out (STOP Commands)

You can withdraw your SMS consent and opt out at any time. Your opt-out request will be processed immediately:

Text Commands (Immediate Opt-Out):

  • STOP - Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe immediately
  • UNSUBSCRIBE - Alternative unsubscribe command
  • QUIT - Alternative unsubscribe command
  • END - Alternative unsubscribe command

Other Opt-Out Methods:

  • Email us at: contact@hsyed.dev with "SMS OPT-OUT" in subject
  • Contact us through our website: www.hsyed.dev
  • Reply to any SMS with your opt-out request

Opt-Out Confirmation

Once you opt out, you will receive a confirmation message and no further SMS messages from hsyed.dev unless you explicitly opt in again through our website.
